This section covers various hardware (mostly) modifications that extend the creative lifespan of my favorite little subnotebook (Belarusian Wiki page [here](https://be.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lenovo_ThinkPad_X220))

## Various adapters expanding laptop functionality

1) [Adapter](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:ExpressCard_to_M.2_2230_or_2242_NVMe_SSD_adapter_front_and_back_views.jpg) enabling connection of NVMe SSD drives (2230 and 2242 form factors) to the laptop and booting from them (using UEFI payload from coreboot BIOS or external bootloader like Clover).
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/fc/ExpressCard_to_M.2_2230_or_2242_NVMe_SSD_adapter_front_and_back_views.jpg" width="600" alt="EC2M2U adapter front and back views">

2) [Adapter](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Intel_Wi-Fi_6_AX200NGW_card_with_Mini_PCIe_to_M.2_adapters.jpg) for connecting modern mini-PCIe WiFi 6/7 M.2 form factor cards to the laptop. The external cable allows placing the card in a convenient (free) location under the chassis.
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/61/Intel_Wi-Fi_6_AX200NGW_card_with_Mini_PCIe_to_M.2_adapters.jpg" width="600" alt="Mini PCIe to M.2 adapters">

3) RF pigtail [adapter](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:RF_pigtail_adapter_MHF4_male_to_U.FL_female_30_mm.jpg) MHF4 male to U.FL female allowing use of new M.2 standard WiFi cards with the laptop's standard antennas.
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/4f/RF_pigtail_adapter_MHF4_male_to_U.FL_female_30_mm.jpg" width="600" alt="MHF4 male to U.FL female">

4) [Adapter](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:M.2_%26_mSATA_dual_SSD_to_SATA_adapter_PCB_with_hardware_switch.jpg) with hardware switch enabling connection of M.2 & mSATA SSDs and switching between them.
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/34/M.2_%26_mSATA_dual_SSD_to_SATA_adapter_PCB_with_hardware_switch.jpg" width="600" alt="M.2 and mSATA dual SSD to SATA adapter">

5) [Adapter](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:M.2_dual_SSD_to_SATA_adapter_PCB_with_hardware_switch.jpg) with hardware switch enabling connection of 2x M.2 SSDs and switching between them.
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/da/M.2_dual_SSD_to_SATA_adapter_PCB_with_hardware_switch.jpg" width="600" alt="2x M.2 SSD to SATA adapter">
